My Life and Times

By Willard Carroll Smith

In West Philidelphia, born and raised, on the playground is where I spent most of my days:

  • Chilling out
  • Maxing
  • Relaxing, all cool

I was shooting some b-ball outside of school when a couple of guys -- they were up to no good -- stared making trouble in my neighborhood

I got in one little fight and my mom go scared. She said,

''You'll move in with your Auntie and your Uncle in Bel-Air''
